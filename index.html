I'm not going to pitch you anything. I just want to tell you who we are, in plain words, before you fill this in.

House of Velara is a sisterhood. Not a brand. Not a subscription. Not a club where you pay and get a tote bag. It is a group of real women who show up for each other — at events, in our community group, in each other's lives.

I built this because I was burned out and alone in the middle of a life that looked fine on the outside. What I needed wasn't another app or workshop. I needed sisters. Women who got it without me having to explain.

This form is not a sign-up form. It is a hello. It takes about 5 minutes. Fill it in honestly — especially the last question. I read every single answer personally. That is not something I say lightly.

And if you're reading this at 11pm with a cup of tea, procrastinating on something, wondering if you'll really fit in here — you will. That feeling is exactly why this place exists.

Welcome home. ✦
